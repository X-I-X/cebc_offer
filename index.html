Plus ($500/month): Everything in Essential, plus up to 4 hours per month of content updates handled by our team (event page updates, photo swaps, copy changes, minor design tweaks).

Full ($600/month): Everything in Plus, plus bug fixing, integration adjustments, small feature additions, and priority response within 24 hours.
